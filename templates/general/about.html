About Chargebee

Chargebee is a comprehensive, enterprise-grade billing and invoicing platform designed specifically for service providers and modern businesses. It bridges the gap between complex financial operations and an intuitive user experience, enabling seamless end-to-end financial management.

Our platform handles everything from QR code-powered instant payments and automated invoicing to real-time inventory tracking and robust analytics. Chargebee empowers both administrators and clients through dedicated portals, ensuring full transparency, efficiency, and real-time synchronization across all your business functions.

Key Features & Capabilities

QR Code & Payment Gateway

  • Auto-generated QR codes on every invoice for instant mobile payments
  • Integrated Razorpay payment gateway with real-time status tracking
  • Scan-to-pay functionality with automatic payment reconciliation
  • QR codes embedded in downloadable PDF invoices and email notifications

Invoicing & Billing

  • Professional invoice generation with GST/tax calculations
  • Downloadable PDF invoices with company branding & QR codes
  • Automated email delivery of invoices to customers
  • Partial payments, payment history tracking, and customer statements

Inventory & Product Management

  • Real-time stock tracking with low-stock & out-of-stock alerts
  • Manual stock adjustments and damaged item removal tracking
  • Automatic inventory deduction upon invoice generation
  • Product catalog with unit pricing, HSN codes, and GST rates

Analytics & Reporting

  • Interactive dashboard with revenue trends, collection rates, and P&L
  • Visual charts for invoice trends, top products, and top customers
  • Expense tracking with categorization and business reports
  • Exportable business reports for financial analysis

Client Portal & Communication

  • Dedicated client login with restricted Data Access Control
  • Clients view only their invoices, payments, and spending metrics
  • Threaded feedback & communication on individual invoices
  • Automated payment reminder notifications via email

Administration & Security

  • Role-based access control (Admin, Staff, Client)
  • Complete system activity logging with login/logout history
  • Company branding settings with logo, address, and GST details
  • Dark mode support and collapsible sidebar navigation

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

1. Data Collection

Chargebee collects minimal personal and business information strictly necessary to facilitate billing, invoicing, and account management. This includes names, company details, email addresses, and encrypted payment history.

2. Data Usage

Information gathered within Chargebee is utilized specifically to process transactions, generate financial documentation (invoices, receipts, reports), and to provide customer support or communications via the built-in messaging threads.

3. Data Security and Third Parties

All data is secured within our private database instance. Chargebee integrates with third-party payment gateways such as Razorpay. We do not store full credit card numbers or backend bank credentials, relying instead on secure tokens provided by our licensed gateway partners.

4. Client Access Restrictions

Clients logging into Chargebee are securely restricted via Data Access Control to view ONLY the invoices and transaction histories directly linked to their assigned Customer Profiles.
